Get Started with Privnote

What is Privnote?

Privnote is an online platform that allows you to send private messages that automatically delete themselves once they have been read. It's the perfect solution for those who need a secure, temporary way of sharing sensitive information like passwords, personal notes, or confidential documents.

How Does Privnote Work?

Using Privnote is incredibly simple. Here’s how you can send a self-destructing secret message:

  1. Visit the Privnote website.
  2. Write your secret note in the provided space.
  3. Set the self-destruction timer to delete the message once opened.
  4. Enter the recipient's email address to receive a read notification.
  5. Click "Send" and share the generated link with your recipient.

Features of Privnote

Privnote's Key Features
  • Self-Destructing Messages: Once your recipient opens the note, it disappears forever.
  • No Registration Required: Simply write your message, and Privnote takes care of the rest.
  • Read Notifications: Receive an email when your recipient opens your note, allowing you to track when your message was read.
  • Secure and Private: Privnote ensures end-to-end encryption and data privacy.
  • No Storage of Notes: Once a note is opened and deleted, it’s gone for good. No trace remains.

Use Cases for Privnote

Privnote is ideal for a variety of scenarios where confidentiality is key:

  • Sending passwords and access codes.
  • Sharing confidential documents and business information.
  • Sending personal or romantic notes securely.
  • Sharing legal or financial information with clients or colleagues.

Get Notified When Your Note is Read

One of the standout features of Privnote is the ability to receive email notifications when your note has been read. Simply enter the recipient's email address when creating the note, and Privnote will send you an alert once the message is opened.
