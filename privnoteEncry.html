Powerful Encryption Tools

Privnote.chat provides cutting-edge encryption methods to secure your messages and ensure that they remain private. Our features include zero-width character encryption, encrypted link sharing, and binary encryption, offering a range of options to keep your information safe.

Zero-Width Character Encryption

Convert your message into zero-width characters, embedding it in ordinary text that can only be decoded using special tools. This method provides advanced privacy protection, ensuring that your message is concealed even in plain sight.

Encrypted Link Sharing

Generate an encrypted link containing your message and share it with others. You can set an expiration time for the link, and it will self-destruct after being read, ensuring that your message is only available for a limited time.

Binary Encryption

Encrypt your text into binary code (0s and 1s), adding an extra layer of security. Even if the message is intercepted, unauthorized access will be impossible without the decryption key.

  • Temporary File Sharing: Use self-destructing links or expiration settings to ensure your files are deleted after being read.
