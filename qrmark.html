How Do I Prevent Someone from Forwarding My PDF?

MaiPDF provides advanced security features that help you control how your PDFs are shared and viewed, ensuring that only authorized users can access them.

1. DynamicQR for Added Security

The DynamicQR embedded in your PDF link contains crucial information such as the user's device details, IP address, and file-specific metadata. This helps you trace who opened the file and from which device or location, making unauthorized sharing more difficult.

2. Set Access Limitations

You can configure a total open limit for your PDF link. Once the specified number of views is reached, the link will become inactive, preventing further access. This adds a layer of control to ensure the document doesn't get shared beyond your intended audience.

3. Modify the File After Sharing

Even after you've sent the PDF link, MaiPDF allows you to modify the file's content or change the settings (like the open limit) at any time. This feature ensures that you can update or revoke access if needed, adding an extra level of control over your document.

4. Real-Time Monitoring

With DynamicQR, you can track who is accessing your PDF and when. This helps you monitor the usage of your document and ensures that it is not being forwarded or shared without your permission.
