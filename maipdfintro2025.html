How MaiPDF Works

Upload PDF

Simply upload your pre-password-protected PDF files. No need to set passwords on the platform—your PDF remains as it is.

Dynamic Watermark

Each PDF gets a dynamic watermark in the form of a QR code. The QR contains access details like time, IP, and browser information for tracking.

Secure Access

Track who accesses your document with unique watermarks and control access based on IP, time, and device details.

How to Use MaiPDF

1. Upload Your Password-Protected PDF

Go to MaiPDF’s website, click the “Upload” button, and select the PDF files that are already password-protected.

2. Generate Dynamic Watermark

MaiPDF automatically generates a dynamic watermark for each PDF, containing unique details about the access, such as the user's IP, time, and browser.
