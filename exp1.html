Why You Should Set an Expiry Date on Your PDFs

Setting an expiry date on your PDF files ensures that your confidential documents are not accessible after a certain time, preventing unauthorized access and protecting sensitive information. Whether for business contracts, private information, or other important documents, MaiPDF offers a simple and secure way to manage your PDFs with self-destruct functionality.

How It Works

Step 1: Upload Your PDF

Simply upload your PDF document to MaiPDF's secure platform. We support password-protected files for added security.

Step 2: Set Expiration Date

Choose the date when your PDF file will expire. After the set time, the document will no longer be accessible.

Step 3: Share Your PDF

Generate a secure link or QR code and share it with the intended recipients. The link will automatically expire after the set date.
