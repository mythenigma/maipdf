MaiPDF's Online and Offline DRM Options

Offline DRM: Secure, No Installation

MaiPDF converts your PDF into an HTML file that works across all browsers. It includes encryption and tracking features similar to expensive DRM solutions like Adobe DRM or Locklizard PDC. Users don’t need any additional software to view the document.

The file will automatically expire after the set time, and all actions, including viewing and printing, are securely controlled.

Online DRM: Easy to Share

With MaiPDF’s online DRM, you can generate a secure web link or QR code to share your document. The link has built-in restrictions like view limits, time constraints, and disabling copy, print, and download features.

Once the document’s access period has ended, it will be inaccessible, providing complete control over your shared files.
