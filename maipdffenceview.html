MaiPDF's Fence View: Protecting Your PDFs with Enhanced Security

MaiPDF's Fence View adds an extra layer of protection to your PDFs by overlaying visual barriers over the content, making it difficult for unauthorized users to capture or screenshot your documents.

When a PDF is opened with MaiPDF's Fence View, the content is obscured by vertical lines. The content is revealed when you hover over the fence, offering a seamless reading experience for authorized users.

This unique feature prevents screenshots by overlaying fences, disrupting traditional screen capturing methods and enhancing your document's security.

MaiPDF's Fence View is ideal for protecting confidential documents, intellectual property, and sensitive data in industries such as legal, financial, and healthcare.
