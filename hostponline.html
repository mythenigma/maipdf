What is Hosting a PDF Online?

Hosting a PDF online means uploading your PDF file to the cloud and generating a shareable link that allows others to view, download, or interact with the document. MaiPDF offers a hassle-free solution to upload and manage your PDF documents securely, ensuring that you have complete control over who accesses your content.

MaiPDF’s online hosting feature enables you to host and share your PDFs effortlessly, with full control over how your files are accessed, shared, and protected.

How Does MaiPDF's PDF Hosting Work?

With MaiPDF, you can upload your PDF files directly to the cloud and generate a unique link to share the document. Here’s how it works:

  • Upload Your PDF: Simply upload your PDF file to MaiPDF’s secure cloud platform.
  • Generate a Shareable Link: Once your file is uploaded, a unique link is created, which you can send to anyone you want to share the document with.
  • Set Access Restrictions: You can control who can access the document and whether it’s downloadable, printable, or viewable only in the browser.
