Privnote Chat: Complete Guide and Feature Overview

Creating a New Note

Privnote.chat provides two text boxes: the top box is for public text, and the bottom box is for hidden, encrypted content.

Encryption Methods

Upon clicking "Encrypt Note," two encryption types appear simultaneously:

  • Zero-Width Character Encryption: Invisibly embeds secret messages within regular text.
  • Binary Encoding: Converts hidden messages into binary (0s and 1s).

Generating and Sharing Secure Links

Secure links generated by Privnote.chat can self-destruct immediately after reading or after a specific period (1 hour, 4 hours, 24 hours, or 7 days).

Important: Self-destruct applies only to the link, not the encrypted content itself. Content can still be saved and decoded later.

Decoding and Retrieving Encrypted Content

You can decode encrypted content anytime, even after link expiry, by revisiting Privnote.chat and using the decoding tool provided.
