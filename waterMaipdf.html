MaiPDF: Securely Host and View Password-Protected PDFs

MaiPDF allows you to upload your pre-password-protected PDF files. When shared, the PDF will require the user to enter the password to view it. MaiPDF displays the PDF file as it is without modifying its content. Additionally, MaiPDF creates dynamic watermarks in the form of a QR code, containing access time, user IP, and browser information to track any unauthorized sharing or screenshots.

Introduction

MaiPDF is a simple and secure platform for hosting your PDFs. You can upload files that are already password-protected and choose to generate dynamic watermarks in the form of QR codes. These QR codes will contain valuable information like the time of access, IP address, and browser details.

Key Features
  • Upload password-protected PDFs without needing to set a password through the platform
  • Dynamic QR codes act as watermarks, tracking access details (time, IP, browser)
  • Maintain full control over your PDFs with password protection and QR-based watermarking
  • Track access and deter unauthorized sharing by embedding unique access information in each QR code

How to Use MaiPDF

Follow these simple steps to get started with MaiPDF:

  1. Go to the MaiPDF website.
  2. Click the "Upload" button to select your already password-protected PDF files.
  3. Click "Upload" to begin sharing your files securely with the generated watermark.
  4. Once uploaded, click "Share" to generate a secure shareable link or QR code for others to access the PDF.
