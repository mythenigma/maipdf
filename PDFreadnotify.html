Get Notified When Your PDF Is Read

With MaiPDF’s ReadNotify feature, you will receive real-time email alerts when someone opens your PDF link, providing you with instant tracking of your document’s access.

1. Instant Email Alerts

Whenever someone clicks on your PDF link, MaiPDF automatically sends an email to the document creator. This email includes the time and details of the access attempt, so you can always stay informed about who is viewing your document.

2. Full Document Access Tracking

MaiPDF doesn’t just track when a document is opened, but also records important information like the device used, the browser, and the IP address. This gives you comprehensive insight into who is accessing your file.

3. No Extra Setup Required

Setting up ReadNotify is incredibly easy. Once your PDF is uploaded and converted into a shareable link, the email notifications are automatically enabled. You don't need to configure any additional settings or software.

4. Monitor Document Access in Real-Time

The ReadNotify feature offers real-time monitoring. You can check your email immediately after someone opens your PDF, keeping you informed as the document is shared and read by others.
