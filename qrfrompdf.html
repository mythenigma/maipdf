What is PDF Link Generation?

PDF link generation allows you to share your PDF documents securely with others. Instead of sending the actual PDF file, you generate a secure link that can be accessed by the recipient. This feature provides better control over who can view your document and prevents unauthorized access.

By generating a link, you can easily share the document while maintaining its integrity and security, without worrying about file tampering or unauthorized copying.

How PDF Link Generation Works

When you generate a PDF as a link, the document is hosted on a secure server, and a unique URL is created. You can then share this link with specific recipients, who can access the document directly through the link. This method has several key advantages:

  • Control Access: You can limit the number of views or accesses the link can have, ensuring that your document isnâ€™t shared beyond the intended audience.
  • Secure Sharing: Sharing a link is more secure than sending attachments via email, as it ensures the file remains in a controlled environment.
  • No Download Required: The recipient can view the document online without downloading it, reducing the risk of unauthorized distribution.
